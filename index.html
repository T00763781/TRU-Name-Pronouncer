About Use the tool

Your name matters

This tool helps you create a quick, shareable pronunciation guide. It’s a starting point: adjust it to match what you prefer. When in doubt, ask the person — they’re the source of truth.

Privacy

Runs in your browser — nothing is uploaded.

Use case

Great for email signatures, LMS profiles, introductions, and class rosters.

Respect-first

Prefer asking over guessing. Small effort, big belonging.

Accessibility

All controls are keyboard accessible. If you embed this tool, keep the surrounding copy — it’s part of the inclusion practice.
